## 2. Ko-fi Support & Integration Plan

To support ongoing development without imposing paywalls on core functionalities, we propose an elegant "Support / Tip" model.

### Key Goals:
- Keep the extension 100% open-source and free.
- Offer voluntary support options (Ko-fi tips).
- Provide minor visual perks for supporters (e.g., custom themes or extra avatar frames).

### Ko-fi Placement in UI:
- **Location:** At the bottom of the Settings/Inspector panel and the footer of the `README.md`.
- **Badge Style:** A clean, matching SVG badge: `Buy Me a Coffee / Support on Ko-fi`.
- **Text Copypasta (EN):**
  > ☕ **Love LoreRelay?**
  > LoreRelay is a free, local-first open source project. If it has enhanced your TTRPG and text-adventure sessions, consider supporting us on Ko-fi! Every coffee keeps the engine running and helps us add features like advanced VLM integrations.
- **Text Copypasta (JP):**
  > ☕ **LoreRelay を気に入っていただけましたか？**
  > LoreRelay は完全無料でオープンソースのローカルファースト開発プロジェクトです。もしこの拡張機能でTRPGやテキストアドベンチャーが楽しくなったら、Ko-fi（コーヒー代のサポート）を通じて応援していただけると非常に励みになります！
